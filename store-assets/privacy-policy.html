Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Knockit ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

When you use the Prayer Times feature, the App requests access to your device's GPS location. This location data is used solely to calculate Islamic prayer times for your geographic coordinates. Location coordinates are transmitted to the aladhan.com API and are not stored on our servers or associated with your identity in any way.

1.2 Reminder Data

All reminder content you create (titles, schedules, repeat settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

1.3 AI Input Text

When you use the natural language reminder input feature, the text you type is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. This text is used only to parse your reminder intent and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store the text on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate and display accurate Islamic prayer times based on your location
  • To interpret natural language reminder requests via AI processing
  • To schedule and deliver local push notifications on your device

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond core app functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — Natural language processing for reminder creation. Google Privacy Policy
  • aladhan.com Prayer Times API — Islamic prayer time calculation. Aladhan Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as required for app functionality.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All reminder data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual reminders within the App
  • Uninstalling the App from your device
  • Clearing app data via Android Settings → Apps → Knockit → Clear Data

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Security

All data stored on-device is protected by Android's built-in application sandboxing. API communications use HTTPS encryption.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
