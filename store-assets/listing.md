## Full Description
Never forget what matters most. Knockit uses AI to turn your everyday language into smart, reliable reminders — no complex setup required.

Just say what you need:
"Remind me to take my medication every day at 8am"
"Drink water every 2 hours"
"Call mom every Sunday at noon"

Knockit understands you, sets everything up, and keeps you on track.

---

🤖 AI-Powered Natural Language Input
Type reminders the way you speak. Knockit's built-in AI (powered by Google Gemini) understands natural language and automatically configures the time, frequency, and content of your reminder — no menus to navigate.

🕌 Prayer Times (Salah Reminders)
For Muslim users, Knockit includes automatic Islamic prayer time reminders based on your GPS location. Get accurate Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha reminders wherever you are in the world.

⚡ 7 Quick-Start Reminder Templates
Not in the mood to type? Tap a quick-start scene and you're done:
• 🍼 Baby Feeding — track infant feeding schedules
• 🕌 Prayer Times — auto-detect salah times by location
• 💊 Medicine — daily medication reminders
• 💧 Drink Water — hydration reminders throughout the day
• 🏃 Exercise — workout and movement reminders
• 😴 Sleep — bedtime and wake-up reminders
• ⏰ Custom — create any reminder you can imagine

🔁 Flexible Repeat Options
• One-time reminders
• Daily reminders
• Weekly reminders
• Interval reminders (every N minutes or hours)

🔔 Reliable Local Notifications
All reminders use local push notifications — no server required, no internet needed for delivery. Your reminders fire on time, every time.

🌙 Beautiful Dark Theme
Sleek, dark-first UI that's easy on the eyes day and night.
